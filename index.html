Mercedes-Benz G-Class

The Mercedes-Benz G-Class has 1 Diesel Engine and 2 Petrol Engine on offer. The Diesel engine is 2925 cc while the Petrol engine is 5461 cc and 3982 cc . It is available with Automatic transmission.Depending upon the variant and fuel type the G-Class has a mileage of 8.13 to 11.8 kmpl & Ground clearance of G-Class is 241 mm. The G-Class is a 5 seater 6 cylinder car and has length of 4606 mm, width of 1976 mm and a wheelbase of 2850mm.
